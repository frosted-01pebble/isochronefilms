Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Isochrone Films LLC operates this website. We take privacy seriously. This site is a static preview page — we do not run a store, collect signups, or maintain user accounts. As a result, we collect very little information about you, and we do not sell any of it.

This policy describes what limited information is collected when you visit this site, how it is used, and what your rights are.

1. What We Collect

We do not operate contact forms, newsletter signups, or any mechanism that asks you to submit personal information directly. We do not set first-party cookies or run advertising trackers.

However, some information is collected automatically through the third-party services that make this site function:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time on site
  • Referring URL

This information is collected by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and our typeface delivery service (Google Fonts) as part of their standard operation. We do not control what they collect or retain.

2. How We Use It

Isochrone Films LLC does not use your information for advertising, profiling, or marketing of any kind. To the extent we access any usage information, it is used only to understand how the site is performing and to maintain it.

3. Third-Party Services

This site relies on two third-party services, each of which has its own privacy practices:

  • GitHub Pages — site hosting. GitHub may log requests made to their servers, including your IP address. See GitHub's Privacy Statement at github.com/privacy.
  • Google Fonts — typeface delivery. When your browser loads fonts from Google's servers, Google may collect certain information. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

We are not responsible for the data practices of these third parties.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Information

Isochrone Films LLC does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for monetary or other valuable consideration. We have no advertising relationships and no data broker arrangements.

5. Cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies. Google Fonts may make network requests that your browser handles as part of loading the page. You can configure your browser to block third-party requests or cookies at any time — this will not affect your ability to use the site.

6. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information is collected about you, the right to request its deletion, and the right to opt out of its sale. Because we do not directly collect or sell personal information, most of these rights apply primarily to our third-party service providers rather than to us. We will make reasonable efforts to respond to any privacy request you submit.

To submit a request, contact us using the information in Section 8 below.

7. Children

This site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us or through a service on this site, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be directed to Isochrone Films LLC. We will respond as promptly as possible.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

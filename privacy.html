Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026

Overview

Isochrone Films LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates isochronefilms.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of this site. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable law, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

1. Information We Collect

This site is a static informational website. We do not operate contact forms, account registrations, or e-commerce. As a result, we do not directly collect personal information from visitors.

However, certain information may be collected automatically through third-party services we use to host or analyze the site, including:

  • IP address and general geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring URL
  • Device type and operating system

This information is collected by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and any analytics tools we may use, and is subject to their respective privacy policies.

2. How We Use Information

To the extent any information is collected, it is used solely to understand how visitors interact with the site and to maintain and improve site performance. We do not use any collected information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

3. Cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies. Third-party services (such as Google Fonts, which we use to load typefaces) may set cookies or make network requests as part of their standard operation. We do not control these third-party cookies. You may configure your browser to reject cookies at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • GitHub Pages — site hosting (github.com/privacy)
  • Google Fonts — typeface delivery (policies.google.com/privacy)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

5. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including:

  • The right to know what personal information is collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale of your personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. As this site does not directly collect personal information, we have limited ability to fulfill access or deletion requests, but we will make reasonable efforts to respond to any such requests.

To submit a privacy request, contact us at the information below.

6. Do Not Sell My Personal Information

Isochrone Films LLC does not sell personal information. No opt-out is required, but if you have questions about how your data is handled by third-party services on this site, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data directly. Any data retained by third-party services is subject to their own retention policies.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact Isochrone Films LLC via the contact information listed on this site.
